About Me

Design that’s both beautiful and useful

I’m a graphic designer based in Karachi, Pakistan, with a strong focus on Canva template design and brand identity. My interest in design started with a simple question: why should good design be complicated to use? That question still shapes every project I take on.

My process starts with understanding the purpose behind a design — who it’s for, what it needs to say, and how it will actually be used — before a single element is placed. From there, I build around clear grids, considered typography and colour palettes that feel intentional rather than decorative.

I care about the details most people scroll past: consistent spacing, aligned elements, and type that’s easy to read at a glance. Branding especially excites me, because it’s where strategy and visual style meet. My goal with every template, deck or brand system is the same — make it practical, make it beautiful, and make it effortless for the person using it.

Great design isn’t decoration — it’s clear communication.
